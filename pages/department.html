About the Department

Our Department of Engineering & Technology stands at the forefront of innovation and academic excellence, dedicated to shaping the next generation of engineers and technology leaders.

With a vision to advance technological innovation and foster critical thinking, our mission is to provide world-class education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical application, preparing students for the challenges of tomorrow.

We offer cutting-edge programs in Electronics, Computer Science, Chemical Engineering, and Artificial Intelligence, each designed to equip students with industry-relevant skills and deep technical knowledge.

Our programs are important because they bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world application, ensuring graduates are ready to lead innovation in their respective fields and contribute meaningfully to society's technological advancement.

Through state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry partnerships, we create an environment where curiosity meets opportunity, and passion transforms into expertise.

Branches Offered

Explore our diverse engineering programs designed for the future

Electronic & Computer Engineering

Master the art of electronics, embedded systems, and computer architecture. Learn to design circuits, microprocessors, and cutting-edge hardware solutions.

Why it matters: ECE professionals drive innovation in IoT, telecommunications, and smart devices that power our connected world.

VLSI Design Embedded Systems Signal Processing Microcontrollers

Chemical Engineering

Study chemical processes, material science, and industrial production. Design sustainable solutions for energy, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing.

Why it matters: Chemical engineers solve critical challenges in sustainability, healthcare, and resource management for a better planet.

Process Design Thermodynamics Catalysis Bioengineering

Computer Engineering

Blend hardware and software expertise. Learn computer architecture, operating systems, and system-level programming to build powerful computing systems.

Why it matters: Computer engineers create the foundation for all modern technology, from smartphones to supercomputers.

System Architecture Embedded Programming Computer Networks OS Development

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science

Explore machine learning, deep learning, big data analytics, and intelligent systems. Build AI models that learn, predict, and make decisions.

Why it matters: AI & DS professionals are revolutionizing every industry with data-driven insights and intelligent automation.

Machine Learning Neural Networks Big Data NLP
