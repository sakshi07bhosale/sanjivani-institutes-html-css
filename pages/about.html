About Sanjivani College of Engineering

Shaping Engineers of Tomorrow with Excellence and Innovation

Our Vision

To become a leading engineering institution nurturing innovation, creativity, and leadership among students.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality technical education with ethical values.
  • Encourage research, innovation, and entrepreneurship.
  • Develop globally competent engineers contributing to society.

Our History

  • 1983: Sanjivani College of Engineering was established with a commitment to quality education.
  • 1995: Introduced postgraduate programs in engineering.
  • 2005: Recognized for excellence in technical research and student achievements.
  • 2020: Accredited by national bodies and ranked among top engineering colleges.

Message from Our Principal

“At Sanjivani College of Engineering, we aim to create an environment where students grow academically, socially, and ethically. We focus on innovation, practical learning, and preparing future leaders who can make a positive impact in the world.”
